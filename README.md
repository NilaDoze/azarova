## The Archives

The archive section contains breakdowns of behavioral and psychological patterns such as:

* Emotional dynamics in relationships
* Patterns of control, guilt, silence, and avoidance
* Mechanisms of identity distortion
* Behavioral responses under stress
* Archetypal personality patterns

Each entry is connected to ideas explored in my YouTube videos.



## Behavioral Assessment

The quiz is designed to map behavioral patterns based on:

* Core fears  
* Emotional responses  
* Psychological wounds  
* Coping strategies  
* Identity structure  

It outputs a personality pattern profile based on recurring behavioral logic — not just surface traits.
